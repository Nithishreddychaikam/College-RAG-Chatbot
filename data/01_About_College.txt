About ABC Institute of Technology

ABC Institute of Technology is a fictional engineering institution established in 2008 with the objective of providing quality higher education in engineering, technology, management, and computer applications.

The institute is affiliated with a recognized State Technical University and approved by AICTE.

Campus Location

The college is located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, in a peaceful educational environment with excellent road connectivity.
