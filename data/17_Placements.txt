Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell at ABC Institute of Technology prepares students for successful careers through continuous training and campus recruitment activities.

Placement Process

1. Registration
Final-year students register with the placement cell.

2. Pre-Placement Training
Students receive:
- Aptitude training
- Logical reasoning
- Quantitative aptitude
- Verbal communication
- Soft skills
- Resume preparation
- Group discussion practice
- Mock interviews

3. Company Visits
Recruiting companies visit the campus and conduct pre-placement talks.

4. Selection Process
Depending on the company, students may undergo:
- Online assessment
- Coding test
- Technical interview
- HR interview

5. Job Offer
Selected students receive offer letters through the placement cell.

Internship Opportunities

Students are encouraged to complete internships from the second year onwards.
