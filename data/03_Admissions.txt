Admissions

Admissions to ABC Institute of Technology are conducted through national and state-level entrance examinations as well as merit-based counseling.

Eligibility depends on the course selected and the regulations of the affiliated university.

Required Documents:
- 10th Marks Memo
- 12th/Diploma Marks Memo
- Transfer Certificate
- Study Certificates
- Aadhaar Card or Government ID
- Passport Size Photographs
- Entrance Examination Rank Card
- Income Certificate (if applicable)
- Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Students can apply online through the admission portal or visit the admission office for guidance.

The admission office assists students with counseling, document verification, fee payment, and course selection.
