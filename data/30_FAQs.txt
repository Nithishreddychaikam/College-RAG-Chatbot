Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What courses are offered?
A. The college offers B.Tech, MBA, and MCA programs.

Q. Is hostel accommodation available?
A. Yes. Separate hostels are available for boys and girls.

Q. Are scholarships available?
A. Yes. Government, merit, sports, minority, and EWS scholarships are available.

Q. Does the college provide transport?
A. Yes. GPS-enabled buses operate from nearby towns and cities.

Q. Is placement assistance available?
A. Yes. The Training and Placement Cell provides placement training and campus recruitment support.

Q. Does the campus provide Wi-Fi?
A. Yes. The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled.

Q. Is there a library?
A. Yes. The library contains books, journals, e-books, and digital learning resources.
