Rules and Regulations

Students are expected to maintain discipline throughout the campus.

Important Rules:

- Wear College ID Card at all times.
- Maintain minimum 75% attendance.
- Ragging is strictly prohibited.
- Respect faculty, staff, and fellow students.
- Mobile phones should not be used during classes.
- Maintain cleanliness inside the campus.
- Smoking and alcohol consumption are strictly prohibited.
- Students should protect college property.

Violation of college rules may result in disciplinary action.
