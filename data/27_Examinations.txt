Examination System

The college follows the academic regulations prescribed by the affiliated university.

Evaluation Pattern

The evaluation consists of:

- Internal Assessment Tests
- Laboratory Examinations
- Assignments
- Mini Projects
- Semester End Examinations

Attendance Requirement

Students must maintain at least 75% attendance to become eligible for semester examinations.

Hall Tickets

Hall tickets are issued before every semester examination.

Grading System

Students are evaluated based on credits and grade points as per university regulations.

Malpractice Rules

- Mobile phones are prohibited.
- Any form of malpractice leads to disciplinary action.
- Students must carry their hall ticket and ID card during examinations.

Supplementary Examinations

Students who fail a subject may appear for supplementary examinations conducted by the university.
