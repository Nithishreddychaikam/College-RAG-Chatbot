Transport Facilities

ABC Institute of Technology operates a well-organized transportation system for students and staff.

Features:
- GPS-enabled college buses
- Experienced drivers and support staff
- Regular maintenance of buses
- Fixed pickup and drop points
- Safety monitoring during travel

Coverage:
College buses operate from nearby cities, towns, and villages.

Students can choose transport services during admission or at the beginning of every academic year.

Transport Fee:
The annual transport fee ranges from ₹18,000 to ₹35,000 depending on the distance from the college.

Students must carry their transport ID card while boarding the college bus.
