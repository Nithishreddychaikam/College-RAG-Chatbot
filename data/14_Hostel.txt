
ABC Institute of Technology provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls within the campus. The hostels offer a safe, secure, and comfortable environment for students.

Hostel Features:
- Separate hostels for boys and girls
- 24×7 security with CCTV surveillance
- Biometric entry system
- Wi-Fi connectivity
- Spacious furnished rooms
- Reading and study halls
- RO purified drinking water
- Laundry facilities
- Housekeeping services
- Indoor games room
- Television room
- Medical assistance
- Backup power supply
- Solar water heaters

Mess Facilities:
- Hygienic vegetarian and non-vegetarian food
- Breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner
- Weekly special menu
- Nutrition-focused meals

Hostel Rules:
- Students must carry their ID cards.
- Hostel timing must be followed.
- Ragging is strictly prohibited.
- Visitors are allowed only during visiting hours.
- Students are expected to maintain cleanliness and discipline.

Hostel Fee:
₹60,000 per academic year including accommodation and mess.
